Who Are We.

We are one the fastest growing recruitment services and human resources solution provider firm in Gujarat.

Shivalic HR Solution mostly focuses on entry level to execute level positions in all verticals. Shivalic HR Solution maintains their transparency & confidentiality at clients as well as at candidates.

With our strong candidate data base in various functions and locations, we are committed to deliver the result within demanding time as per your requirements. We believe that our team work and relationship are as a backbone of our strength.

Our Process.

Our organization is indulged in providing HR Consultancy Services to our clients. These are used for providing human resource service for managing organization services. This include HR Process Consulting Service, HR Management Services and HR Policy Development Services.

Our Approach.

We focuses on people management programs and long-term solutions, and stresses organizational development interventions, achieving employee organizational fit, and other aspects that ensure employees add value to the organization.

Our Goal.

We make possible that you get associated with the best talent as per your requirement.

Our Mission.

Our Mission is to provide leadership role and provide service in support of Company's vision by promoting the concept that our employees are our most valuable resource and will be treated as such.
