Android Developer

We are hiring Android developers who will be responsible for the development and maintenance of applications aimed at a vast number of diverse Android devices. Candidates will be involved in the development of Android applications and their integration with back-end services.

Job Description.

  • Design and develop quality Android Applications
  • Maintain existing Android Applications
  • Must write highly scalable, testable, and maintainable code
  • Discover and fix bugs

Required Experience, Skills and Qualifications

  • Required 1 year experienced Android Developer [ FRESHERS ALSO CAN APPLY ]
  • Strong knowledge of OOP Concept and Java Programming
  • Good knowledge in Android Studio and Debugging with Android Studio
  • Must have experience with REST API, JSON Parsing, Async Task, Google Map, Retrofit, Google Volley
  • Must have experience in Social Media Integration, Firebase Integrations, and Third-Party Library Integration

Education Qualification

  • M.Sc. IT, MCA, BE Computer Science, BE IT, B Tech or equivalent

Budget:

  • Up to 7K – 15K
